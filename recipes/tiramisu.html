Tiramisu Recipe

Tiramisu garnished with some sort of earthy powder.

This tiramisu recipe is pretty okay. It's not terrible, it's not great, it's the perfect dessert for dinners with friends and family who just can't help putting you down to make themselves look better. When you just can't be asked, slide this across the table and just think about winning the lottery.

Ingredients

  • Whipping cream
  • Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Espresso
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Cookies
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Add espresso and coffee liqueur to a bowl and mix.
  2. Add cheese, sugar, and vanilla to the cream in another bowl and really mix.
  3. Dip the cookies in the coffee.
  4. Put a cookie on a plate.
  5. Spread the cream over the cookie.
  6. Put a cookie on the cream.
  7. Spread the cream over the cookie.
  8. Take a palm-full of cocoa powder and clap your hands over the plate.
  9. Brace yourself, company is coming...
