Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna garnished with fresh cherry tomatoes and organic lettuce.

This lasagna recipe is easy, easy, easy! All you have to do is throw these ingredients into a pan, slide it into that oven, and wait 7 minutes. Presto! No prep, no heat, no nothing. You'll wonder why anyone would ever trust us.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Pasta sauce
  • Pasta cheese
  • Pasta spirit
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh salt from the Dead Sea

Steps

  1. Empty that pan of all your cooking utensils.
  2. Cover the bottom with a thin layer of oil.
  3. Toss in a sheet of pasta.
  4. Cover the sheet with a generous layer of sauce and cheese.
  5. Repeat 3 and 4 until you run out of either.
  6. Sprinkle some salt in arbitrary unmeasured amounts.
  7. Slide it into the oven with some pasta spirit.
  8. Wait 7 minutes with the oven set at room temperature [OFF].
  9. Question everything.
  10. Please like and subscribe! ❤️
